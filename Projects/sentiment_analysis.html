10-K Filing Sentiment Analysis

Project Overview

Analysis of sentiment measures derived from 10-K filings and their correlation with stock returns for S&P 500 companies.

Key Findings

  • LM and ML sentiment measures show significant correlations
  • Growth sentiment strongly associated with returns
  • Results differ from Garcia et al. (2022) due to sample differences
